**What's on screen:** Dashboard "Live Mode" — idle clip of AI avatar in TikTok Shop chrome, fake viewer count rising from 0, fake chat empty.

**What I say:**

I'm going to walk into this audience, grab any item one of you is holding right now, and before I finish talking about it, an AI avatar will be selling that exact object live. Nothing pre-rendered for your item. Nothing scripted. Here we go."

**Prep check:** Dashboard idle loop playing, viewer counter ticking. **Pre-warm** the lip-sync queue with a throwaway dummy render right at 0:05 so API is hot when we actually need it at 0:30.

## Beat 1 — Grab the item + film (0:20 → 0:45)

**Action:** Walk 3-4 steps into audience, pick an audience member in front row, ask: *"You — can I borrow whatever's in your hand for 20 seconds? Promise I'll give it back."* (Practiced phrase — rotate target based on whatever looks safest.)

**Film:** Hold my phone steady, pan around the item once slowly, narrate naturally:
> *"So this is [thing the audience member is holding]. It's [color]. I can see [visual detail]. Here's [feature]. Real-world price is probably [guess]. [Any brand marking they point out]."*

~10s of narration. Phone auto-uploads to backend on stop.

**On screen (during filming):** Dashboard stays on idle loop. Audience is watching ME, not the screen, which is what we want.

**Talk-track while phone is uploading (sec 40-45):**
> "That video is uploading to my laptop now. The intake pipeline's running — transcribing what I just said, analyzing the frames. Watch the screen."

**What I say (5-10s only):**

## Critical orchestration details

1. **Pre-warm:** dashboard fires a dummy render to the lip-sync API the moment I click "Go Live" at 0:05. This warms the queue so by 1:00-ish when the real render fires, cold-start latency is gone.
2. **Intro/bridge orchestration:** Single state machine on the dashboard:
   - `WAITING` → (upload received) → `INTRO_PLAYING` (stateless generic intro)
   - `INTRO_PLAYING` → (intro ends AND pitch not ready) → `BRIDGE_PLAYING` (first bridge)
   - `BRIDGE_PLAYING` → (bridge ends AND pitch not ready) → `BRIDGE_PLAYING` (next bridge)
   - `BRIDGE_PLAYING` → (pitch ready AND current bridge ending) → `PITCH_PLAYING` (crossfade)
   - `PITCH_PLAYING` → (pitch ends) → `IDLE_PLAYING` (loop silent idle)
3. **Hallucination guard:** Claude Haiku script must include ≥2 concrete visual descriptors from transcript OR image analysis. Rejected scripts → re-prompt once → fall back to generic template.
4. **Upload timeout:** 3s hard cap on phone→laptop upload. If stalled, QuickTime stage capture as backup path.
5. **Network speedtest:** Phase 5 rehearsal includes a speedtest at venue. If <10 Mbps upload, switch to ethernet tether.

## Failure mode responses

| Failure | What I say | What happens |
|---------|------------|--------------|
| Upload stalls | "Let me swap networks, one sec." | QuickTime capture of same video on stage laptop, pipeline re-runs |
| Intake fails (Deepgram/Claude error) | Keep talking the market beat | Fallback: play backup pre-recorded demo video |
| Lip sync renders >45s | Chain bridges: "let me check it out... oh wait, look at this..." | State machine automatically chains up to 3 bridges |
| All else fails | "Look — it's live, sometimes the internet isn't. Let me show you a recording." | Play backup screen-recording of a rehearsal run |
